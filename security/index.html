// Vulnerability Disclosure

CyberSecElite, LLC welcomes responsible reports that help keep our properties and clients secure. Please share enough detail to reproduce the issue and avoid actions that could impact availability or privacy.

  • Email: diorjuraev@cybersecelite.com
  • PGP available on request for sensitive submissions.
  • Include: affected asset, steps to reproduce, impact, and any logs or screenshots.

// In Scope

  • Public assets owned by CyberSecElite, LLC (e.g., *.cybersecelite.com).
  • Client targets only when there is explicit, written authorization from the client and CyberSecElite, LLC.
  • Findings that demonstrate real security impact (auth bypass, data exposure, RCE, injection, etc.).

// Out of Scope

  • Denial of service, spam, or automated scanning that degrades availability.
  • Social engineering against CyberSecElite staff or clients.
  • Physical security testing.
  • Clickjacking on pages without sensitive actions, low-impact missing headers, or best-practice suggestions without a demonstrable risk.

// Testing Guidelines

  • Use non-destructive methods; avoid exploiting the same issue repeatedly once proven.
  • Do not access, modify, or exfiltrate data beyond what is necessary to demonstrate impact.
  • Respect rate limits and avoid production disruption.
  • Coordinate with us before testing any third-party integrations.

// Response Targets

  • Acknowledgment: within 3 business days.
  • Initial triage: within 7 business days (impact assessment and next steps).
  • Remediation timelines vary by severity; we will keep you updated on progress.

// Safe Harbor

If you follow this policy and act in good faith, we will not pursue legal action for your research. Please provide reasonable time for remediation before public disclosure.
